On a keyboard:
Up and down arrows for moving through menus, and defending attacks
Space to select on the main menu, and to skip cutscenes.
R to restart while in game.
M to go back to the Menu.
ESC to quit the game.

On a gamepad:
X to mute the game.
A to skip cutscenes and continue text or select option on main menu.
Y to restart while in game.
B to go back a screen/exit from main menu.
Right bumper to make a new hint
Left Joystick to move around menu and block
Back to quit the game.
